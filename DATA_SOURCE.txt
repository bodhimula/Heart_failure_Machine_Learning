DATASET DESCRIPTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------
This dataset contains heart disease diagnostic information collected 
from various clinical parameters such as age, sex, chest pain type, 
resting blood pressure, cholesterol, fasting blood sugar, resting ECG, 
maximum heart rate, exercise-induced angina, oldpeak, ST slope, and 
the presence of heart disease as the target variable.

The dataset is commonly used for predictive modeling and educational 
purposes related to cardiovascular disease risk prediction.
